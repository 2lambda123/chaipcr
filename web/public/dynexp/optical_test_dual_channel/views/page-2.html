STEP 1/7

Prepare the Tubes

25uL Water
25uL FAM
25uL HEX
TO BEGIN:
Prepare 4, 8-well PCR strips. In two of these strips, pipette 25uL of water into each tube. In the other two strips, pipette 25uL of Fluorescein Calibrator into each tube. It is important to pipette the calibration solution from the same stock tube. If the solution is pipetted from different stock tubes, the stock solution should be first be mixed.
