Optical Test

The optical test verifies the proper functioning of the dual channel Open qPCR's optical system.

DURATION
~5 minutes
MATERIALS REQUIRED
6x 8-well PCR strips
200uL Water [25uL/tube]
200uL 3 uM FAM [25uL/tube]
200uL uL 3 uM HEX [25uL/tube]
*It is important to pipette the caliration solution from the same stock tube. If the solution is pipetted from different stock tubes, the stock solution should first be mixed.
