Open qPCR Optical Calibration

The optical calibration procedure corrects well-to-well differences in Open qPCR's optical path, allowing data to be compared between wells. The calibration should be performed whenever the lid height is adjusted, the make of PCR tubes is changed, or the Open qPCR is transported.

DURATION
~30 minutes
MATERIALS REQUIRED
4x 8-well PCR strips
200uL Water [25uL/tube]
200uL Chai's Open qPCR Calibration Solution [25uL/tube]
*It is important to pipette the caliration solution from the same stock tube. If the solution is pipetted from different stock tubes, the stock solution should first be mixed.
